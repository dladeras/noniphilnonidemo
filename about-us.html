About Us

The Ocean shores where Noni naturally grow is the rich source of trace minerals, nutraceuticals, phytochemicals and vitamins mined by the deep roots of the small Morinda citrifolia Linn. Tree that bears the Noni fruits, the source of PhilNONI fruit juice. The inscription Philippine Morinda Citrifolia, Inc. is the registered name of the Philippine company that produces PhilNONI. PMCI is an Agro-Industrial Manufacturing company registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in 2001.

Mission

"Making Philnoni available, accessible and affordable to millions of people anytime, anywhere."

Vision

"Healthier, happier more productive life for millions of men, women and children in the world."
